About Us

Sahid Smriti Awasiya Secondary School, is a public academic institute located in Tulsipur Dang district of Nepal. This school is affiliated with the (NEB) National Examinations Board and approved by Ministry of Education (MoE), Nepal. It offers Ten plus Two (10+2) programs in Humanities, Management, and Education stream.

Sahid Smriti Awasiya Secondary School, Dang provides the basic to Ten Plus Two (10+2) level education with moderate fees structures. It provides scholarships schemes for deserving, hardworking and the poor students of the society.

School provides various facilities such as extracurricular activities (ECA), Educational Tour, Conference, Sports, Counseling, Classrooms, Library, and scholarships facilities.
